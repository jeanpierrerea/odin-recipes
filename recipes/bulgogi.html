Bulgogi

A staple of Korean barbecue, bulgogi translates to “fire meat” (a direct link to the tradition of making it by grilling marinated beef over charcoal).

Recipe Source

Ingredients:

  • Boneless pork
  • Trimmed Hanger Steak
  • Boneless Short Rib
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Brown sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Combine sauces, oil, and sugar in a bowl
  2. Slice meat into thin strips
  3. Add sliced meat into marinade
  4. Slap meat into skillet, tossing and turning it to cook!
  5. Serve over rice!
