Jerk Chicken

What is Jerk Chicken?

Jerk Sauce is the blend of the African and European influences that represent Jamaica. Anchovy sauce, tamarind paste, garlic and onions are actually the basis of British condiment Worcestershire Sauce which was created in 1836.

Recipe Source

Ingredients:

  • 2 organic chicken leg quarters
  • Red Palm Oil
  • anchovy sauce
  • tamarind paste
  • tbsp jamaican allspice
  • thyme
  • hot pepper sauce

Steps:

  1. Massage the chicken in oil
  2. Slam all the pepers
  3. Combine all the ingredients
  4. Bake the chicken
  5. Grill the chicken
